About this website

Description

Diabetes Health Tracker is a platform built to assist diabetes patients and their doctors to record relevant data and monitor health status. This platform bridges connections between patients and doctors as patients can update their diabetes-related health data and comments including blood glucose level, weight, doses of insulin intake, excercise to the platform on a daily basis. And doctors can monitor patient’s health status by viewing the data and comments uploaded by the patients.

Aim

Besides, this platform also aims to provide support and encoragement to diabetes patients. This is achieved by the briged communication between doctors and patients as doctors are encouraged to write a “support message” for their patients. On the other hand, the “leader board” also encourages patient to record their data through displaying the top-5 most engaging users.
