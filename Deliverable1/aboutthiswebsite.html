Description

Diabetes Health Tracker is a platform built to assist diabetes patients and their doctors to record relevant data and monitor health status. This platform bridges connections between patients and doctors.

For patients, they can record and update their diabetes-related health data on a daily basis. These index include blood glucose level, weight, doses of insulin intake, and step count. Patient are also encouraged to write comment on any of these index to the doctor.

On the other hand, doctors can monitor patient’s health status by viewing the data and comments uploaded by the patients. Doctors will enter the safe threshold for each index and the system will emphasize on index fallen out of the safe boundary. Doctors will also write support messages to patient on a daily basis in order to encourage patient to record.



Aim

Besides, this platform also aims to provide support and encoragement to diabetes patients. This is achieved by the briged communication between doctors and patients as doctors are encouraged to write a “support message” for their patients.

On the other hand, the “leader board” also encourages patient to record their data through displaying the top-5 most engaging users. The enaging rate is calculated by the completion percentage of evey day's data record.
